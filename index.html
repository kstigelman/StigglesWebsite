Stiggles SMP

The Convergence Plugin

The Convergence plugin was created by Kyler Stigelman and Kael Hufford for the 2023 Stiggles SMP Server.
Its goal was to enhance the experience of playing Minecraft by adding new twists and ways to adventure.

View on GitHub

Features

NPCs & Shops

Various Non-Playable Characters (NPCs) can be found all around the server world. Many of the NPCs have shops in which players can spend in-game coins to buy in-game items. Coins are collected by defeating monsters.

Quests

Players can complete quests to earn coins and unlock new items in shops. Quests can be initiated by finding hidden items around the world or by interacting with certain NPCs.

Dungeon-Crawler Minigame

Players can choose to enter a dungeon where they may explore a cave, fight monsters, and discover treasure.

Hand-Crafted World

The world was created with adventure in mind. It features vast biomes, numerous pre-built structures to explore, and many secrets to find.
